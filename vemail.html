FAQ
How long until I feel results?
 
Results can vary person-to-person, but generally food and supplements can improve your nutrient levels in 2-4 weeks, hydration can improve in just 1-2 days, and within 1-2 weeks of consistent stress relieving activity, you should be able to see your cortisol levels go down.
 
Should I go with supplements or food?
 
The short answer is both. Nothing beats whole foods. Especially fresh, organic whole foods. But supplements are a nice insurance policy for the times your diet slides off track.
